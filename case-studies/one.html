Women Ethnic Apparel

Explore our case study showcasing outstanding results achieved for a Women Ethnic Apparel brand using Meta and Google Ads in 10 months of time

Approx. 300% increase in orders

Increased no. of orders from 2500/month to 7500/month in from 8l to 22l spends

20% improvement in ROAS with 3 times scale

Improved ROAS 4.8x to 5.8x while scaling up the account aggresively

15% increase in overall AOV

AOV improved from 2250 to 2600- Focusing on high value categories

3 times improvement in Net Revenue

Reduced RTOs, Increased Net Revenue from 58L to 1.55cr

Strategy

Redistribute the spends in separate campaigns and platforms

  • Started Discovery and YT Video campaigns to get cheap audience and build top funnel to scale up further.
  • Redistributed existing Meta spends with multiple Catalog and conversion campaigns, efficient split b//w prospecting and remarketing.

Catalog Optimisation

  • Gradually increased Catalog size from 60 products to 150 with increasing the depth of existing categories and introducing more.
  • Focused spends on hero categories/products. Excluded products with less sizes to increase cart rate.

Identified Target audience and creative ops

  • Experimented with both niche and broad targeting to identify the best possible conversion rates.
  • Tried all types of ad formats combined with various audiences to identify best combinations for the brand.
